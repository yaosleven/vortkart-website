VortKart® was founded in 2009 with a clear mission: to make world-class commercial karting accessible to entrepreneurs, resorts, and entertainment venues globally — without the premium markup of traditional distributors.

Our Journey

From Garage to
Global Leader

2009

The Beginning

VortKart® founded in a 2,000 sq ft workshop with a team of 6 engineers passionate about motorsport. First commercial rental kart model launched and immediately adopted by 3 local tracks.

2012

International Expansion

First export orders shipped to Europe and the Middle East. Manufacturing facility expanded to 15,000 sq ft. Received first ISO 9001 quality certification.

2015

Track Design Division Launched

Expanded services beyond kart manufacturing to include full track design, layout consultation, and turnkey project management — transforming into a complete solutions provider.

2018

Electric Kart Series

Launched the EV-Pro® electric commercial kart series, answering growing demand from indoor venues and eco-conscious operators. Over 200 units shipped in the first year.

2024

Global Leadership

500th kart milestone reached. Operations in 60+ countries. New 40,000 sq ft manufacturing campus opened. Distributor network spans 35 territories worldwide.

Why We Win

Our Differentiators

Three things set VortKart® apart from every other manufacturer in the industry.

01 — Manufacturing

Factory-Direct,
Zero Compromise

Unlike importers or resellers, VortKart® owns the entire manufacturing chain. Our engineers design every component, our welders fabricate every chassis, and our QA team certifies every unit before shipment.

  • In-house steel tube bending and CNC machining
  • Proprietary welding jigs for dimensional accuracy
  • 100-point pre-shipment quality inspection
  • Custom OEM branding and livery production
02 — Design Expertise

Track Design That
Maximizes Revenue

Our in-house track designers don't just draw layouts — they optimize for throughput, guest experience, and operational efficiency. Every track we design is modeled in 3D before construction begins.

  • 3D CAD modeling and simulation for every project
  • Revenue-per-seat-hour optimization analysis
  • FIA-aligned safety barrier and runoff planning
  • Lighting, signage, and spectator zone design
03 — Lifetime Support

Support That
Never Stops

Our relationship with clients doesn't end at delivery. VortKart® operates a global spare parts network and provides dedicated technical support throughout the lifetime of your fleet.

  • 24/7 technical hotline in multiple languages
  • Spare parts shipped within 48 hours worldwide
  • Annual operator training and recertification programs
  • Fleet upgrade and refurbishment services
